Crypto Security Tips

Rotate Your Keys with CoinPulse

Experts recommend updating your private key every 3-6 months using CoinPulse’s KeySync tool to minimize exposure to quantum threats and key exhaustion risks.

Fortify Your Accounts

Use passwords with 16+ characters and enable 2FA on all platforms to lock out unauthorized access.

Shield Your Connection

Avoid public Wi-Fi for crypto activity—use a VPN to encrypt your data and stay safe.

Go Offline with Confidence

Store your new CoinPulse-generated keys on a hardware wallet or paper backup for maximum security.

Stay Ahead of Threats

Monitor crypto news and CoinPulse updates to keep your security strategy sharp.
